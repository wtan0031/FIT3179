EDUCATION IN 21st CENTURY

In the 21st century, the Internet has played an important role when comes to education. With the born of the Internet, there are more and more useful information spread online, and hence surfacing the new term - Online Education. During the pandemic, the whole world is in lockdown, hence the only way for the student to gain information is via Online Education. Teacher's Education

Schools require many different input to produce educated individuals and teachers are arguably the most fundamental one. Research has shown the teacher who has received higher education will have a more positive impact on the student's achievement.

Evidence suggests that teachers who have earned advanced degrees have a positive impact on high school mathematics and science achievement when the degrees earned were in these subjects.

Importance of Education

Research from the United States has shown that the higher education attainment of an individual has will lead to a higher salary . One of the reasons is higher education individuals will have relevant knowledge and skills for high paid jobs such as University Lecturer, Scientist, and Doctor.

Besides, research also showed that higher education individual has a lower unemployment rate because the supply for the lower education individual is so high that the employer could easily hire another employee to replace a current employee.
